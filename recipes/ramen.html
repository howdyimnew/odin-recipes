Ramen

Descripion

A easy and fast dish, that is loved world wide!

Ingredients

  • ramen
  • spices
  • eggs
  • ham

Steps

  1. get a pan and add some water
  2. put the ramen in and let it heat
  3. add the spices
  4. if all the water is gone before ramen are done add some more
  5. shorty before all the water is gone add the eggs and ham
